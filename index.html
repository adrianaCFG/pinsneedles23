🪡 Hand Embroidery Workshop 🪡
Flowers 🌷 March 14th

£25 each session or £42 if you sign up for both. Suitable for beginners

Come and join us for a really creative and rewarding hand embroidery workshop, learning how to create beautiful flowers and leaves to embellish your creations with.

Hand embroidery is a versatile, decorative art used to personalize, mend, and enhance fabric items.

Popular uses include customizing clothing, creating home decor (framed hoops, throw pillows, tea towels), and crafting accessories like patches, bookmarks, and jewelry. It is also used for personalized gifts, quilts, and visible mending.
